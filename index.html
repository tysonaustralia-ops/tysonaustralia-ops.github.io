Frequently Asked Questions

What is SelfMade?

SelfMade: Idle Billionaire Tycoon is a free idle tycoon game for iOS and Android. You start with nothing, build businesses, invest in the stock market, buy real estate, and collect luxury lifestyle assets — private jets, superyachts, and private islands — as your passive income empire grows.

Is SelfMade free to play?

Yes. SelfMade is completely free to download and play on both iOS and Android. The core game — building businesses, investing, and acquiring lifestyle assets — requires no purchases.

Does SelfMade work offline?

Yes. SelfMade is an idle game, which means your businesses and investments continue earning passive income even while you're not playing. Come back after hours or days and collect everything you've earned.

What kind of game is SelfMade?

SelfMade is an idle clicker game and incremental simulator in the tycoon genre. It combines business management, stock market trading, real estate investment, and a luxury lifestyle simulator into a single mobile game.

What can I do in SelfMade?

You can build and upgrade businesses across multiple industries, trade stocks and crypto on a live in-game market, invest in real estate properties around the world, buy luxury assets including private jets, superyachts, jewellery and fashion, and compete on a global rich list. The game features a full passive income system that grows even when you're not playing.

Is SelfMade similar to other idle tycoon games?

SelfMade shares the satisfying idle business-building loop of the tycoon genre, but goes significantly deeper — with a real-time stock market, real estate portfolio system, crypto trading, luxury asset collecting, and a rich list to climb. It's built for players who want more depth and realism from the incremental genre.
