Cromdale Holdings Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") operates the SelfMade mobile application. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with our app.

SelfMade does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. No account is required. No personal information is requested or stored by us.

Game progress is saved locally on your device only. We do not have access to your save data. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

SelfMade does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for general audiences.
